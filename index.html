Abstract

The Atrial Fibrillation Monitoring Ontology (AFMO) was developed as part of a semantic architecture for personal health monitoring using heterogeneous health data. It is designed to formally represent three categories of data (sensors and sensor observations, personal information, and expert medical knowledge) for AF monitoring.
